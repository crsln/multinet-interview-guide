## Quick Tips for Telling Your Stories

### Before the Interview
1. **Choose 5-7 stories** that cover different themes
2. **Practice out loud** - timing should be 2-3 minutes
3. **Know the numbers** - quantify impact wherever possible
4. **Prepare variations** - same story can answer different questions

### During the Interview
1. **Listen to the question** - make sure your story addresses it
2. **Start with the headline** - "Let me tell you about when I..."
3. **Focus on YOUR actions** - use "I" not "we"
4. **End with the result** - what was the impact?

### What Makes a Good Story

```
STRONG STORY:
- Specific situation with context
- Clear challenge or problem
- Concrete actions YOU took
- Measurable result or learning
- Relevant to the role

WEAK STORY:
- Vague ("we did some optimization")
- No challenge ("everything went smoothly")
- Team-focused ("the team decided")
- No result ("I think it was better")
```
